To make the better world




RAAHM is a social economic initiatve birth out of the burning desire to impact in the global effort to eradicate malnutrition and bring starvation to a zero level in Nigeria.

We are nonprofit team and work worldwide

Child malnutrition is a clinical sign of nutrient deficiency manifested a stunting under weight and wasting. These manifestations are often measured using biomedical or anthropometric indicators. Rapid action against hunger and malnutrition (RAAHM) was conceived out of a burning desire to bring about positive change in the world especially in our dear country by contributing our impact in eradication of hunger and malnutrition in our society.

Our value and purpose is a very important agenda of the sustainable development goals (SDGs). Reducing child malnutrition is a key millennium development goal and a focal policy thrust of the government of Nigeria as well as other countries in sub Sahara Africa.
